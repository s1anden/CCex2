Module 6: Equilibrium and thermodynamics: Relating K and ΔG°


In this module, we consider the relation between the equilibrium state reached by reversible reactions and the thermodynamics of those reactions.

    Summary: One can easily calculate ΔG° from K and vice versa using the equation: K=e-ΔG°/RT. Since these two quantities are related exponentially, seemingly small changes in ΔG° can result in very large differences in K. The next module will explain how K and ΔG° change when you double, reverse, or combine a reaction with another one.
