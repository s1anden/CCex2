Hemoglobin scenario

Chemists use LeChatlier's principle to reason qualitatively about the influence of changes in concentration, temperature or pressure on systems that are at equilibrium. This lecture describes the hemoglobin scenario from a qualitative perspective.

We recommend you (i) watch the lecture (ii) go through the course items that follow, and (iii) watch the Hemoglobin story again. Since the Hemoglobin story uses ideas covered in the library items, the first time you hear the story you may not yet have the background information needed to understand the details.
